CoinVault is a Non-Custodial HD wallet. It ensures the safety of your identities and keys, enabling secure communication and authentication with web apps (Web3 provider).

CoinVault provides functionality similar to Metamask (Ethereum), such as message signing and login on web pages.

With CoinVault, users can manage multiple wallets and accounts, granting them complete control over their assets. The wallet facilitates storage, sending, receiving, and viewing of tokens and NFTs, with future support for smart contract interactions.
